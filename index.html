Lynn & Conor's Wedding

February 20, 2021

Ceremony: 2:00 PM
St. Peter's Catholic Church

Reception: 5:00-8:00 PM
Palm Beach Marriott Singer Island Beach Resort and Spa



Details

Wedding Ceremony

The wedding ceremony will be happening at St. Peter's Catholic Church at 2:00 PM.

Address: 1701 Indian Creek Parkway, Jupiter, FL 33458​​

The ceremony will be indoors with strict CDC-guided hygiene precautions and spaced seating of couples/families together distanced in the pews from others. The bride and groom will be COVID swabbed prior to the big day. All guests must wear a mask while in the church.

For those attending the church ceremony, please be seated no later than 1:45 PM.

For those not attending the church ceremony, we look forward to seeing you at the open air reception afterwards!

Reception

A reception will be happening after the wedding ceremony at the Palm Beach Marriott Singer Island Beach Resort and Spa from 5:00-8:00 PM.

Address: 3800 North Ocean Drive, Singer Island

We look forward to celebrating with an intimate group of family and friends over cocktails and heavy hors d'oeuvres in an outdoor reception at the resort’s ocean view terrace. The open air private venue will have both standing and seating options, and a rain contingency veranda ensures we can celebrate outside.

There will be discounted rate valet parking for the day and night, although we encourage those staying off site to responsibly take rideshare options if needed!

Accommodations

For those desiring overnight accommodations at the reception site, we have a block of 10 rooms reserved at the Palm Beach Marriott Singer Island:

  • 2 one bedroom ocean view ($419/night)
  • 6 one bedroom resort view ($399/night)
  • 2 two bedroom coastal view ($499/night)

Call the hotel reservations department M-F 08:00-17:00 at 1-877-239-5610 and mention the Palmeri-Beardsley hotel room block for the discount, available until Wednesday, January 13, 2021. There are lots of resort amenities and activities- feel free to extend your visit into a mini-vacation. 😀

Other nearby hotel options are available on Singer Island, PGA Blvd, US-1, and the Jupiter Beach Resort and Spa. Feel free to ask us about any specific locations!

Airports

Palm Beach International (PBI) is the closest airport. Second closest is Fort Lauderdale (FLL), followed by Miami International (MIA).
